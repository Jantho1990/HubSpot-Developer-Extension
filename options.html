HubSpot Developer Extension

A long time ago in a Slack Channel far far away, an idea was expressed... nay... an idea was conjured up through a collective discussion of pain points and frustrations on the little things that HubSpot Developers did on a daily basis. Things such as:

  • Too many clicks to get where we needed to be
  • Updates not reflecting due to the great cachining of 2017
  • Memorizing countless parameters
  • A darker UI for those devs who hate bright things
  • Constantly bothering William Spiro and Matt Coley

Through this two developers united forces to help take these items and build one extension... one extension to rule them all. The (Unoffical) HubSpot Developer Extension.

We apologize for the blandness of this screen, but if you'd like to help make it look better, you can help!
